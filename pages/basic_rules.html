the basic rules is a table that compare the player hand to the dealer's. It gives you an advice about the best move to do regarding the odds.

obviously, the odds always favor the dealers. But in blackjack, player can make decision that favor them.

But blackjack is still a game. Playing with your guts is, in my opinion, the best choise. Though, if you don't know what to do in a specific situation, you can always have a look at the basic rules.
